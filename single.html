OUR HISTORY

A DREAM AND A VISION SUBJECTED TO REALITY

Mvono Community Centre (MCC) is a community-based organization in Taita Taveta County that deals with orphans and widows. Unlike other organizations where interested individuals come together and agree on the kind of activities and mode of operation and the benefits, MCC was initiated and established through the direct instruction and leading of the Holy Spirit through one of God’s humble servants, Reverend Violet Chanya.

God audibly called her into this ministry on the 22nd May 2004. God commanded her to go out and visit the orphans and widows, and He gave her the following scripture: James 1:26 – 27 "If any man among you seems to be religious, and bridles not his tongue, but deceives his own heart, this man's religion is vain. Pure religion and undefiled before God and the Father is this, to visit the fatherless and widows in their affliction, and to keep oneself unspotted from the world."

She at first resisted this call considering her financial status (not in any gainful employment) and level of education. What was she to give them? She could not sleep for two months, both day and night, lost appetite and had no peace. She argued with God, but the voice insisted. She finally conceded and God sent her to two homes, identified by name, where she was to see how real orphans and how widows look like.

When she asked what she was to carry and what she was to tell them, God gave her these instructions: “What you eat is what they shall eat. What you wear, they need for wear, if it's money, they also need it. Go with that which you have. Go to the shop and buy 1/4kgs of sugar and tea leaves worth 10 Kenyan shillings.” She was to tell them: “Silver and gold I don’t have, but what I have, I give you.” Then she was to pray together, encourage them in the word of God, comfort them, and tell them that they are in the midst of their brethren.” She went with a friend and saw the conditions of the 2 homes.
